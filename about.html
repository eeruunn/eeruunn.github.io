Who We Are

THE SIFI PHILOSOPHY

The Satyajit Ray International Film Institute (SIFI) was founded on a singular belief: that cinema is not merely entertainment, but a profound reflection of humanity. Inspired by the master himself, we blend the rigorous technical discipline of classical filmmaking with the boundless possibilities of modern digital technology.

We are not just a school; we are a crucible for artists who refuse to compromise their vision. Located in the heart of Kerala, we provide an immersive environment where students are challenged to find their unique voice and tell stories that resonate globally.

Leadership

GUIDING VISION

DR. ARINDAM CHAUDHURI

Chairman, SIFI Trust

"We are building more than an institute; we are building a movement that aims to redefine Indian cinema on the world stage."

An educationist and visionary, Dr. Chaudhuri firmly believes that infrastructure and faculty are the backbone of any world-class institution. His commitment to providing SIFI students with industry-standard equipment and global exposure forms the foundation of the institute's rapid rise to prominence.

PROF. VIKRAM MOTWANE

Institute Director

"Don't just show me what happened. Make me feel it. That is the only mandate we give our students here."

An acclaimed filmmaker known for his narrative depth and visual style, Prof. Motwane brings decades of industry experience directly into the classroom. His hands-on approach ensures that SIFI curriculum remains practical, relevant, and deeply rooted in the art of storytelling.
