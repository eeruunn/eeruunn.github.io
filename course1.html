PROGRAM DETAILS

Course Description

The BA in Cinematography program at Satyajit Ray International Film Institute is designed for students passionate about visual storytelling and the technical and artistic aspects of camera work. This program provides a deep understanding of cinematographic techniques, lighting, camera operation, and visual composition, preparing students for a career in film, television, and digital media.

Whether you're aiming to become a Director of Photography, a camera operator, or a visual storyteller, this degree offers the skills and experience needed to succeed in the competitive film and entertainment industry.

What You'll Learn

This course equips you with hands-on experience in operating industry-standard camera equipment, mastering lighting techniques, and crafting compelling visual narratives. You'll also learn how to collaborate with directors, production teams, and post-production specialists to bring creative visions to life.

  • Master camera operations and composition techniques.
  • Understand and apply cinematic lighting principles.
  • Collaborate effectively with film crews and directors.
  • Build a professional showreel and portfolio for the industry.
