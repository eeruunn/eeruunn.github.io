PROGRAM DETAILS

Course Description

The BA in Film Direction program at Satyajit Ray International Film Institute is crafted for aspiring filmmakers who want to master the art and craft of directing for film and digital media. This immersive program blends creative storytelling with hands-on training in directing, cinematography, script development, and post-production.

Whether you aim to direct feature films, documentaries, or digital content, this degree provides a strong foundation to bring your vision to life and make a mark in the global film industry.

What You'll Learn

In this course, you will gain comprehensive skills in visual storytelling, directing actors, script analysis, and managing film production sets. You'll learn how to lead creative teams, communicate a compelling vision, and execute professional-quality productions from concept to screen.

  • Visual storytelling and directing actors.
  • Script analysis and managing film production sets.
  • Leading creative teams and communicating a compelling vision.
  • Executing professional-quality productions from concept to screen.

"Make impactful decisions that drive success. Lead diverse teams and enhance performance. Cultivate and implement innovative ideas. Navigate global markets and cultural dynamics."
