PROGRAM DETAILS

Course Description

The BA in Audiography & Editing program at Satyajit Ray International Film Institute is designed for students passionate about sound and post-production.

Whether you're interested in working on film sets, sound studios, or editing suites, this program provides essential industry-ready skills.

What You'll Learn

In-depth training in sound recording, mixing, Foley, sound design, and video editing using industry-standard tools.

  • Professional audio recording.
  • Sound & video storytelling.
  • Industry tools exposure.
  • Portfolio development.
